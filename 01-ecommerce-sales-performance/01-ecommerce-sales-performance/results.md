# Results: Ecommerce Sales Performance

## Executive summary
Revenue and orders change over time, and high-value orders make up a meaningful share of total revenue.

- Monthly revenue trend: revenue is highest in ______ and lowest in ______.
- Monthly orders trend: order volume peaks in ______.
- Average order value (AOV): average AOV is about $____ and peaks in ______.
- Product concentration: top products/categories drive a large share of revenue.
- High-value order segmentation:
  - High value (150+): ____ orders, avg order value $____
  - Mid value (75-149): ____ orders, avg order value $____
  - Low value (<75): ____ orders, avg order value $____

## Business implications
- Focus marketing and inventory on the highest revenue products/categories.
- Use promotions to increase mid-value orders into the high-value segment.
- Plan campaigns around months with historically higher order volume.
